Welcome to the Basic Dice Game!

This game is a simple yet fun way to compete with your friends. The rules are straightforward:

1. Player 1 clicks the button labeled "BOX1" to roll their dice. A random number of dots (from 1 to 6) will appear on the dice in Box 1.

2. Player 2 then clicks the button labeled "BOX2" to roll their dice. Similarly, a random number of dots (from 1 to 6) will appear on the dice in Box 2.

3. After both players have rolled their dice, the game will announce the winner based on the number of dots on each player

The player with the higher number of dots wins.

   - If both players have the same number of dots, the game will announce a draw.

Enjoy this quick and exciting game to see who gets luckier with their dice roll!
